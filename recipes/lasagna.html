This is the recipe for lasagna!

Descriptions

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Additional Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 3 hours 15 minutes
  • Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • Meat: lean ground beef
  • Onion and garlic: one onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products: one can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: two tablespoons
  • Spices and seasonings: parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg: one egg

Steps by steps instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe nutrients.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rests before serving.
